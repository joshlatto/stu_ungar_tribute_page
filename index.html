Here's a time line of Stu's life:

  • 1953 Born in New York City
  • 1963 Wins his first gin tournament
  • 1976 Considered one of the best gin players in NYC
  • 1977 Moved to Las Vegas, NV
  • 1980 Wins the main event of the World Series of Poker, becoming the youngest champion to date
  • 1981 Sucesfully defends his title
  • 1997 Wins his final World Series of Poker main event
  • 1998 Dies of a heart condition brought on by years of drug abuse

"Some day, I suppose it's possible for someone to be a better No Limit Hold'em player than me. I doubt it, but it could happen. But, I swear to you, I don't see how anyone could ever play gin better than me."
--Stu Ungar
